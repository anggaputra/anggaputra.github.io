About.

I'm Angga, currently based in Indonesia. I've been designing interaction for digital products since 2009, back then the title was simply just web designer or designer. While working for design agency based in Indonesia, I also learned how to build the HTML/CSS version of my design, from there I build my enthusiasm through technical things, especially front-end development, from web to mobile app development.

Years of experiences in e-commerce, C2C marketplace, B2B SaaS platform. Been through small to large scale product companies as the only designer, team leader or principal designer.

Iterate. Test. Decide

Has been my design philosophy for quite some time every time I start a new project, at the end of the day you need to ship your product and see how it goes while real users interact with it.
