About.

Hi, I'm Angga, currently based in Indonesia. I've been designing interaction for digital products since 2009 (UI/UX), back then the title was simply just web designer or designer, much simpler than nowadays.

When I worked for the design studio in my early career, I also learned how to build the HTML/CSS version of my designs, from there I build my enthusiasm through technical things, especially front-end development, from the web to mobile app development. I use JavaScript a lot, from creating plugins in Figma to creative coding.

Years of experience in e-commerce, C2C marketplace, and B2B SaaS platform. I've been working for small to large scale product companies, as the only designer, team leader, or principal designer.

Iterate. Test. Decide

Has been my design philosophy for quite some time, it's what I believe most. Why? Because at the end of the day you need to ship your product and see how it goes while real users interact with it.
